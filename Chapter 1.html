Chapter 1: Energy, Nerve & Happy

The Core Loop

Torn revolves around three primary resources: Energy, Nerve, and Happy. Understanding how these interact is the foundation of long-term progression.

Energy - The Engine of Progress

Energy is primarily spent in the gym and is the main source of stat growth.

Nerve - Your Early Game Backbone

Nerve is used for crimes, which provide experience, money, and crime skill progression.

Happy - The Multiplier Most Players Misuse

Happy directly multiplies gym gains. Higher happy results in significantly larger stat increases.

How These Three Bars Work Together

Optimal players manage energy for training, nerve for crimes, and maintain high happy through housing and items.
