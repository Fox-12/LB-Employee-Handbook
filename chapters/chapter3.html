Chapter 3: Resource & Money Management

Contents

    Using Energy and Nerve Properly

    Energy and nerve are the resources that move most early accounts. Energy is usually spent on training, and nerve is usually spent on crimes. Leaving either one full for long stretches slows progress for no good reason.

    In practical terms, this means you should log in often enough to keep both moving. Exact timing will vary by schedule, but the standard remains the same: do not build a routine where your main resources sit unused.

    Spending Priorities

    Early money should improve your account, not just disappear into convenience purchases. Housing, travel access, and the items that support progression come first because they affect what you can do every day.

    This is why a better property, a Private Island with an airstrip once you can support it, and the tools needed for travel matter more than low-impact spending. Money that improves your baseline is doing real work.

    Banking and Storage

    LB members can use the faction bank, and bankers are available through the faction announcement or Discord. That gives you a practical way to store money, but you still need to handle it carefully and understand who to contact when you need access.

    The larger point is simple: protect your cash, keep enough on hand for training and travel, and avoid putting yourself in a position where one bad loss stops your progress for the week.
