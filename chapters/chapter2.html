Chapter 2: Requirements, Setup & Communication

Contents

    Requirements

    LB expects members to meet minimum activity standards. The current baseline is 30 drugs per month, 100 crimes per month, and participation in chains and wars when the faction is active. These numbers are there to set a floor, not to define an ideal ceiling.

    The point of requirements is reliability. Organized crimes, chaining, and war preparation all depend on members being active often enough that leadership can plan around them.

    Setup

    Three tools are required after joining: TornStats, YATA, and Discord. TornStats is used for growth tracking, spy reports, and general account visibility. YATA serves a similar practical role and is part of the faction's expected setup. Discord is required because it is the fastest place for announcements, coordination, and questions that need an answer now.

    Setup should be handled early. Delaying these steps makes it harder to communicate, harder to track progress, and harder for the faction to trust that a member is actually plugged in.

    Communication

    Faction chat is used daily, but it is not the only place you are expected to look. Rules, updates, absence notices, and instructions may appear in announcements, forum posts, or Discord. Members are responsible for keeping up with those channels.

    Real life comes first. If you are going to be away for an extended period, tell leadership or post in the away thread. Silence causes confusion. Notice lets people work around it.
