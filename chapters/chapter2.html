Chapter 2: Requirements, Setup & Communication

Contents

    Faction Requirements

    LB expects members to meet the faction minimums, stay active, and take part in required faction activity. That includes monthly drug usage, crime activity, and participation in chains and wars when expected.

    Daily Expectations

    Use your main resources regularly, check faction communication, and stay aware of what is currently going on. Daily consistency matters more than occasional high effort.

    Real Life and Inactivity

    Real life comes first. If you are going to be away for an extended period, let leadership know through the usual faction channels so the absence is understood rather than assumed.

    Required Setup: TornStats

    TornStats is required because it helps track growth, compare progress, and support faction coordination. It is one of the main tools used to understand how your account is developing.

    Required Setup: YATA

    YATA is also required. It serves a similar purpose in helping members use better tools, organize information, and stay connected to the systems the faction relies on.

    Required Setup: Discord

    Discord is required. It is the main place for broader communication across members and related groups, especially when timing and coordination matter.

    Where to Find Rules and Updates

    Members should know where faction rules, announcements, and guide links are kept. Read those first, then ask questions when something still needs clarification.
