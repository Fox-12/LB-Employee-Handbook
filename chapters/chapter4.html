Chapter 4: Early Progression (Levels 1–15)

Contents

    The Goal of the Early Game

    Before level 15, the main job is to become strong enough to move into the next stage of the game. Travel is the major unlock because it gives access to a reliable income method that is difficult to match at lower levels.

    This is why level holding is not allowed before level 15 in LB. You are not gaining anything by delaying that unlock.

    How to Reach Level 15

    The usual approach is straightforward. Train until you can beat suitable targets comfortably, then use attacks that give experience efficiently. If a target is too strong or your wins are messy, go back to training and try again later.

    This is not a chapter about squeezing every point of experience out of the system. It is about getting through the early gate cleanly and without wasting time on side projects that do not solve your immediate problem.

    What Changes After 15

    Once travel opens, the purpose of leveling changes. You do not need to keep forcing levels the same way, and your attention can move toward stats, travel income, and account setup.

    At that point, a Large Suitcase and a Private Island with an airstrip become practical priorities because they improve how much you can carry and how efficiently you can fly.
