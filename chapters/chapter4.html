Chapter 4: Early Progression (Levels 1–15)

Contents

    Why You Should Not Hold Levels

    Before level 15, leveling up should happen as soon as it becomes available. Reaching travel quickly is too important to delay without a specific reason.

    How Leveling Actually Works

    Early leveling is usually done by training enough to beat known leveling targets, then attacking and leaving them in the street for experience rather than mugging them.

    Building Enough Stats First

    If you cannot beat common targets cleanly, your first job is not to force more attacks. Train more, come back later, and level efficiently once your account is ready.

    Reaching Level 15 Efficiently

    The main goal of early progression is unlocking travel. Once that happens, your money options improve, and your account starts opening up much faster.

    What Changes at Level 15

    At level 15, travel becomes available, and that changes your priorities. After that point, you usually stop actively chasing levels and put more attention into growth systems that scale better long term.

    What Not to Overfocus On

    Do not get distracted by trying to optimize every small thing before travel is unlocked. The early goal is straightforward: get to 15 cleanly and without wasting momentum.
