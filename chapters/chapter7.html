Contents

    Crimes as a Daily System

    Crimes are not just filler activity. They are part of your monthly requirement, part of account development, and one of the steady uses for nerve. If you ignore them, you fall behind in more than one area at once.

    The goal early on is not to force every high-risk option for quick cash. It is to keep building crime progress without wrecking your own consistency.

    Income Before and After Travel

    Before travel opens, money options are narrower. Reselling city items and other small methods can help, but they do not solve the larger income problem by themselves.

    After travel opens, income becomes more reliable because you can import items and sell them in Torn. That shift is why early leveling has a direct financial purpose.

    Meeting Requirements Without Slowing Down

    LB requires monthly crimes and monthly drug use, but the point is not to clear a checklist at the last second. The easier way to stay compliant is to build them into your normal routine.

    A routine is easier to maintain than a scramble. If you handle crimes steadily, the requirement takes care of itself.
