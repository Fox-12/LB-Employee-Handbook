Chapter 1: What Torn Is

Contents

    Overview

    Torn is a long-term game where your account develops over time. There are no resets, and progress is permanent.

    At the start, your account has limited options. Stats are low, income is inconsistent, and many parts of the game are not fully available yet. As you progress, more systems open up and your account becomes easier to manage.

    The early stage is mainly about getting out of that limited position and into something stable.

    Progression and Early Direction

    Progress comes from using the core resources available to you. Energy is used to train or attack. Nerve is used for crimes. Money is used to improve your setup, including housing and travel. Early on, these are limited, so how you use them matters.

    Your first goal is to become functional. That means training enough to win fights consistently and leveling up until travel is unlocked. Once travel is available, you gain access to a reliable way to earn money by buying items abroad and selling them in Torn.

    After that point, your focus changes. Leveling matters less, and more of your progress comes from stronger stats, steadier income, and cleaner use of your resources.

    What This Guide Focuses On

    This guide is built to keep things in the right order. It focuses on what matters at each stage without trying to cover everything at once.

    The goal is to help you avoid early slowdowns, understand what to prioritize, and build your account in a way that still makes sense later.
