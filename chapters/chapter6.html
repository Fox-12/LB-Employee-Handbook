    Why Stats Come First

    Your stats determine what you can safely attack, how easily you can level early, and how useful you are in many faction activities later. That makes training one of the core jobs of any growing account.

    Even when another goal is more visible, such as travel or company perks, weak stats usually become a limiting factor.

    Happy and Training Quality

    Housing matters because happiness affects training, especially on smaller accounts. A poor property gives poor results. A better property improves the value of the same energy.

    This is why cheaper short-term options can end up costing more in progress. If your setup is weak, your training is weaker than it needs to be.

    Training With a Plan

    Good training is not random. You should know what stat spread you are trying to build, what gym you are working toward, and whether your current setup supports that work.

    You do not need an advanced formula to start. You do need consistency, enough happy to make your training worthwhile, and enough discipline to keep your energy focused.
