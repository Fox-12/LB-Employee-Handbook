Contents

    Why Travel Matters

    Travel is where many accounts stop struggling for money. It opens access to flowers, plushies, and other foreign items that can be brought back and sold for profit in Torn.

    This does not make every flight equally good, but it does give you a repeatable way to generate income once your setup is ready.

    Setting Up Travel Properly

    The standard setup is a Private Island with an airstrip and a Large Suitcase. The island improves travel efficiency and item capacity, and the suitcase adds more space on top of that.

    Without those upgrades, you can still travel, but your returns are lower and your routine is less efficient.

    Trading With Discipline

    Travel money looks simple because the loop is simple: fly, buy, return, sell. The discipline comes from doing it consistently, checking item value, and not tying up your cash in bad choices.

    This is one of the clearest examples in Torn of a system working well when your setup is in order. Good travel habits produce reliable cash flow.
