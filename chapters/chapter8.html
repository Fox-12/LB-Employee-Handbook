Chapter 8: Travel & Trading (Level 15+)

Contents

    Why Level 15 Matters

    Travel is one of the biggest early turning points in Torn because it opens a practical money path that is easy to repeat and easy to understand.

    Private Island Setup

    A private island with an airstrip improves travel efficiency and usually becomes one of the first major setups worth paying for once travel is unlocked.

    Large Suitcase

    A large suitcase increases carrying capacity and improves the value of each trip. It is useful, but usually comes after the travel-enabling setup itself.

    Plushies and Flowers Basics

    Most early travel income comes from buying items abroad and bringing them back to Torn to sell at a profit. Plushies and flowers are common starting points because they are simple and well understood.

    Scaling Travel Income

    Travel income improves with better setup, stronger habits, and more efficient routing. The more active and organized you are, the more value you can usually pull from it.

    Travel as a Long-Term Support System

    Even when your account grows beyond basic travel income, travel often remains a useful support tool rather than something you leave behind completely.
