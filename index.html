    Welcome to LB

    Legitimate Business is built around active members, clear expectations, and steady improvement. The faction is meant to help newer players get established, but it also expects members to use that support properly. Reading, following instructions, and staying involved are part of the job.

    The point of this handbook is to give members one place to start. Instead of learning everything through scattered forum posts, chat replies, and partial advice, you can work through the pages in order and understand what matters first.

    How LB Operates

    LB expects members to be present, communicate, and make use of the tools provided to them. Real life comes first, but unexplained absence causes problems for organized crimes, chains, wars, and general planning. If you are going to be away, say so.

    This faction also expects basic self-management. Read the available material before asking the same question repeatedly, use TornStats and YATA after joining, and keep up with faction announcements, rules, and Discord.

    How to Use This Handbook

    The handbook is arranged in the same order most players experience the game. It begins with a basic explanation of Torn, moves into setup and requirements, then covers resources, progression, income, travel, jobs, faction activity, and long-term growth.

    You do not need to memorize everything in one sitting. What matters is that you can return to the right chapter when a part of the game becomes relevant. Treat this as a working reference, not a one-time read.
