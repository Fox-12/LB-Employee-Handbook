Joining LB: Onboarding & Identity

Welcome to Legitimate Business. This handbook is built to give members a clear starting point, explain how LB operates, and make the rest of Torn easier to understand.

The home page covers onboarding and identity. The chapters begin with Chapter 1, which explains what Torn is and how progress works.

Contents

    What LB Is

    LB is a faction built around steady growth, participation, and long-term progress. The goal is not to look active for a week and disappear after that. The goal is to build good habits, stay involved, and keep moving forward over time.

    What Kind of Player This Is Built For

    This handbook is written for newer players, returning players, and members who want a clear structure instead of learning everything through trial and error. It is meant to be practical and readable, not exhaustive.

    How We Approach Progress

    Progress in Torn comes from consistency more than short bursts of effort. Members are expected to use their resources, take part in faction activity, and keep improving over time. That matters more than trying to force quick results.

    Expectations vs Typical Players

    Many players waste resources, ignore useful tools, and drift without a plan. LB expects the opposite. Members should know the basics, use available tools, read guides before asking repeated questions, and take part in the parts of faction life that keep everyone moving.

    Using Available Help

    There are experienced players here who can help, but the first step is always to use what is already available. Read the guides, check the tools, and then ask better questions if you still need help. That saves time and makes answers more useful.

    Handbook Layout

    The rest of this handbook moves in order. Chapter 1 explains what Torn is. After that, the guide covers requirements, setup, resources, progression, mistakes to avoid, and the systems that matter most as you grow.
