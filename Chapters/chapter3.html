Chapter 3: Resource & Money Management

Contents

    Energy

    Energy is one of the most important resources in Torn because it directly affects stat growth and many other actions. Letting it sit capped is one of the fastest ways to waste progress.

    Nerve

    Nerve supports crime progression and part of your income path. It should be used regularly and with some purpose rather than ignored for long periods.

    Avoiding Resource Waste

    The main idea is simple: do not leave valuable resources unused. Missed regeneration is progress you do not get back later.

    Early Spending Priorities

    Early money should support growth. That means housing, travel setup, training support, and practical upgrades before cosmetic or low-impact purchases.

    Reinvestment Strategy

    Money is most useful when it makes future progress easier. The best early spending decisions usually improve your income, your training, or your general efficiency.

    Using the Faction Bank

    The faction bank is there to help members store and move money more safely when needed. Use it correctly and understand who the approved bankers are before relying on it.

    Working With Bankers

    If you need funds moved, ask the listed bankers through the proper channels. Keep requests clear and use the systems the faction already has instead of improvising your own.
