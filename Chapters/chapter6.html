Chapter 6: Stat Growth

Contents

    How Stats Actually Grow

    Stats grow mainly through repeated gym use over time. Bigger gains come from consistency, good happy, and using energy effectively.

    Happy and Gym Gains

    Happy matters because it improves training gains, especially earlier on. Better housing and certain jump methods make a noticeable difference during the early game.

    Early Gym Strategy

    Early on, the important part is not finding the perfect advanced build. The important part is training regularly enough that your account keeps moving forward and can handle the targets and content in front of it.

    When to Change Training Focus

    Training focus changes as your account develops. At the start, the goal is usually getting strong enough to level and function. Later, focus can shift based on faction roles, preferred builds, and specific goals.

    Why Discipline Matters More Than Perfect Optimization

    A decent plan followed consistently will usually beat a perfect plan followed rarely. Most training mistakes come from inconsistency, not from being slightly suboptimal.

    Where This Connects to the Rest of the Game

    Stat growth supports everything else. It helps with attacks, survivability, war participation, and your general freedom to choose how you want to play.
