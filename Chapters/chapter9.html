Chapter 9: Jobs & Companies

Contents

    Why Player Companies Matter

    Player companies generally offer stronger long-term value than city jobs. Their perks can support training, travel, crimes, income, war, and several specialized playstyles.

    Early Game Company Choices

    Early company choices should match what your account can realistically get into and what actually helps you now. A good entry-level company is better than chasing a late-game option you cannot use yet.

    Mid Game Direction

    As work stats improve, company choice becomes more strategic. At that stage, you can start choosing more intentionally between training support, crime help, income perks, and travel advantages.

    Late Game Overview

    Late-game companies are often more specialized and more competitive. They tend to make sense only when your account, work stats, and playstyle are ready for them.

    Choosing Based on Playstyle

    The best company is not the same for everyone. Good choices depend on whether you care more about growth, energy support, crimes, travel, war, or passive money.

    Using Company Guides Correctly

    Company guides are useful, but they work best as a reference. The point is not to copy every opinion. The point is to understand why a company is good for a certain stage or purpose.
