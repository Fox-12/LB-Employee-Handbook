Chapter 5: Common Early Mistakes

Contents

    Wasting Energy or Nerve

    This is the most common early mistake and one of the most expensive over time. If resources are regularly sitting full, progress is being lost every day.

    Focusing on Money Too Early

    Money matters, but newer players often chase small short-term gains while ignoring the systems that actually grow the account. Growth first usually pays more later.

    Holding Levels Incorrectly

    Holding levels before 15 slows down access to travel and the income that comes with it. Early holding usually hurts more than it helps.

    Ignoring Faction Systems

    Players who do not check faction chat, tools, guides, or requirements usually create avoidable problems for themselves. Use the structure that is already there.

    Not Using Available Tools

    TornStats, YATA, and the internal guide library exist for a reason. Skipping them makes the game harder than it needs to be.

    Trying to Learn Everything at Once

    A better approach is to understand the next system in front of you, use it well, and then move to the next one. Trying to master everything immediately usually creates confusion rather than progress.
